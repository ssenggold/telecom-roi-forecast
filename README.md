# ROI Forecast & Break-Even Analysis

This Excel project breaks down a month-by-month forecast of cumulative profit and tracks when a business hits its break-even point. It’s built to help visualize growth over time and support smarter financial planning.

## Overview

The spreadsheet includes several tabs that focus on different parts of the business:

- **OPEX Summary** – outlines monthly operating expenses
- **Customer Base** – tracks new and total customers over time
- **Customer Revenue** – calculates monthly income from customers
- **ROI Forecast** – shows cumulative profit and highlights when break-even is reached

Everything is connected through formulas so changes in one sheet automatically update the rest.

## Key Highlights

- Tracks how long it takes to become profitable
- Visualizes monthly profit growth in a clear chart
- Easy to update if assumptions or numbers change
- Shows trends in customer growth and revenue
